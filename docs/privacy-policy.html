ScreenGuilty — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

ScreenGuilty is a macOS app that monitors your active application to encourage productivity. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

Data Collection

ScreenGuilty does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

The app uses NSWorkspace to detect the name of the currently active application. It does not:

  • Record screen content
  • Monitor keyboard input
  • Track mouse movements
  • Access files, photos, contacts, or location
  • Connect to the internet
  • Send any data to external servers

Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your Mac using UserDefaults:

  • App classification settings (which apps are "productive" or "distraction")
  • Daily usage statistics (distraction time, productive time)
  • Character size and sound preferences
  • Emotion timing thresholds

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

ScreenGuilty does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Children's Privacy

ScreenGuilty does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
